"A 58-year-old senior manager at a tech company was laid off during a company-wide restructuring. The company subsequently hired a 29-year-old candidate for a nearly identical role at a lower salary. The plaintiff's performance reviews had consistently been rated 'exceeds expectations.' A HR representative informally told him that leadership wanted to bring in 'fresh energy' and 'new perspectives.' The plaintiff believes he was terminated solely because of his age, in violation of the Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA)."
