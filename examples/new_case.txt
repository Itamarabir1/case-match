A 56-year-old federal employee at the USDA alleges age discrimination 
under the ADEA. She claims that older employees were forced out and 
replaced by at least 15 younger, inexperienced employees. She received 
exceptional performance evaluations before 2022. She was subjected to 
adverse employment actions including letters of reprimand, suspension, 
and a proposal for removal. She filed a complaint with the EEOC and 
alleges that the subsequent adverse actions constitute retaliation for 
her protected activity under the ADEA.
